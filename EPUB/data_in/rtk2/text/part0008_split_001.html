Signal Primitives

The following list contains all the signal primitives used in this book, arranged according to number of strokes and primary reading. The numbers refer to the frames in which a given primitive figures in its primary reading. As explained in the text, these primitives do not necessarily coincide with the primitive elements treated in vol. 1.

Tap on any frame number to see the signal primitive to its left. (Note that the ordering moves horizontally to accommodate eBook restrictions.)
